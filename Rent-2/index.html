About Property

This well-presented and bright two-bedroom, two-bathroom apartment (786 sq ft/73 sq m) is located on the first floor of a popular red brick, portered block that retains its original period facade. The apartment features a 22.9 ft reception room, a separate fully fitted modern kitchen, a double bedroom with an ensuite bathroom, a second bedroom, and a shower room. The property benefits from air conditioning, ample storage throughout, and an allocated underground parking space.

Clarendon Court, situated on Maida Vale, offers additional amenities such as CCTV, a passenger lift, and 24-hour porterage. The property provides easy access to a wide range of local cafes, restaurants, shops, and pubs. The green open spaces of Paddington Recreation Ground and Regent’s Park are also nearby.

The area boasts excellent transport links, with convenient access to Warwick Avenue underground station (Bakerloo Line) and Paddington Station, which offers the new Elizabeth Line, National Rail, and Heathrow Express connections.
